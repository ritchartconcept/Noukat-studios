The studio

A creative studio, not just a graphic design studio.

Based in Haiti, Noukat Studios positions itself as the country’s most comprehensive creative narrative studio. It specializes in comics, webtoons, professional illustration, and 2D animation.

In a market dominated by traditional graphic design, Noukat offers a premium, well-structured service focused on modern visual storytelling — bridging the gap between Haitian creativity and international standards.
